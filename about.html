About Zipper

Zipper is a platform that connects food lovers with local home cooks and small-scale food businesses. Our mission is to provide delicious, homemade meals to our customers while supporting local culinary talents.

We believe in the power of good food to bring people together and create a sense of community. By partnering with local cooks, we ensure that you get authentic, high-quality meals that are prepared with love and care.

Our team is dedicated to making the process of ordering and delivering food as smooth and enjoyable as possible. We strive to provide excellent customer service and maintain the highest standards of food safety and quality.
